Meaning of UI Logo

Since the logo of an institution is not only a mark for its legal and public documents, communications, and publications, but more importantly, a symbol of the institution. More than that, it signifies the sentiments and aspirations that guide the institution and its constituents.

It shows an atom in the inner portion of the gear representing the fields of specialization in the University, namely, engineering, science and technology. The ribbon symbolize excellence and quality of education as demonstrated by the rich achievements of the University.

The University's colors are maroon and yellow gold which is the traditional colors of University. It signifies strength, creativity, courage and prosperity.
